Example sentences

The following are some example sentences using Trilangle and its extensions.

"Dupe's mother sun-dried the clothes"

Extensions used
Vocabulary extension (first), None (second)
Idiomatic translation
Dupe's mother sun-dried the clothes.
Literal translation
There exists a thing w such that w is Dupe
and there exists an event e of x being w's mother
and there exists a thing x
and there exists an event f of x drying y
and there exists a thing y that is clothes
and there is an event g of f using z
and there is a thing z such that z is a sun.
Formal semantics
∃w Dupe(w)
& ∃e mother(e, x, w)
& ∃x
& ∃f dry(e, x, y)
& ∃y clothes(y)
& ∃g use(g, f, z)
& ∃z sun(z)
Notes
This is the sentence used for the 2068th Just Used 5 Minutes of Your Day on r/conlangs
