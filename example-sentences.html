Example sentences

The following are some example sentences using Trilangle and its extensions.

"Dupe's mother sun-dried the clothes"

Extensions used
Vocabulary extension (first), None (second)
Idiomatic translation
Dupe's mother sun-dried the clothes.
Literal translation
There exists things w, x, y, and z, and events e, f, and g,
such that w is Dupe,
and e is of x being w's mother
and f is of x drying y
and y is clothes
and g is of f using z
and z is a sun.
Formal semantics
∃w ∃x ∃y ∃z ∃e ∃f ∃g
Dupe(w)
& mother(e, x, w)
& dry(f, x, y)
& clothes(y)
& use(g, f, z)
& sun(z)
Notes
This is the sentence used for the 2068th Just Used 5 Minutes of Your Day on r/conlangs
